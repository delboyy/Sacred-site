Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Sacred, we respect your trust. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when interacting with our website, products, and newsletter.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to deliver your order, personalize your experience, and provide relevant updates. This includes:

  • Contact details such as name and email address when you join our newsletter or submit a contact form.
  • Checkout information gathered by our commerce partners (Gumroad) during product purchases. Sacred does not store full payment details.
  • Usage data like page views, device type, and referral source collected through Google Analytics to help us understand how the site is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver digital products and provide customer support.
  • Send educational content, offers, and updates from Sacred (only if you opt in).
  • Monitor performance, improve our website, and ensure a secure experience.

3. Email Communications

Our email list is powered by MailerLite. Every campaign includes an unsubscribe link, and you can update preferences at any time. We only send emails to people who explicitly opt in via our website or checkout.

4. Payment Processing

Payments and digital delivery are managed by Gumroad. When you check out, you may be asked for your email, billing information, and country or VAT details. Gumroad processes and stores this data under their own privacy policy. Sacred receives limited order information (name, email, product purchased) to fulfill your order and provide support.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use first-party and third-party cookies to measure traffic, improve site performance, and deliver relevant messaging. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact certain features.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent personal data. We share information only with trusted service providers that help us run Sacred (such as analytics, email delivery, and commerce platforms), and only for the services they supply. These partners are required to keep your data confidential and secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, download, or delete your personal information. You can exercise these rights by emailing support@sacred-site.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Security

We use encryption, secure hosting, and restricted access to protect your data. While no online system is 100% secure, we partner with reputable providers and review our security practices regularly.

10. Children's Privacy

Our content and products are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. We will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, when appropriate, notify subscribers via email.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your information, email us at support@sacred-site.com.
